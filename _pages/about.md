Welcome! I'm Siva, an engineer passionate about sustainable energy systems. I hold a B.S. in Electrical Engineering from Purdue University and an MPhil in Engineering for Sustainable Development from the University of Cambridge. 

Previously, I ensured the reliability of the Malaysian grid as an Energy Management Systems Operator at Grid System Operator, Malaysia.

In Fall 2022, I joined the Mays Group @ Cornell University to research the intersection of reliability, sustainability and affordability of electricity markets, exploring how policy and technology can address critical energy challenges.

Outside of work, I enjoy playing table tennis, running trails, and tinkering with electronics. Thanks for visiting!
